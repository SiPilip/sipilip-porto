Project Purpose

The Correctional Institution Archive Management System (SIPAHADI) was conceptualized to solve the critical issue of physical document degradation and slow retrieval times in Bengkulu's correctional facilities.

Prior to this system, archive retrieval could take hours. SIPAHADI digitizes the entire workflow, reducing retrieval time to seconds while ensuring data integrity through strictly role-based access controls. The platform serves administrators, staff, and auditors with a unified, secure interface.
